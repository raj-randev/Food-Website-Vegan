Welcome to LoveVeganFood.co.uk

I built this site because I wanted to provide an open source tool where vegans could use, share and contribute recipes, restaurants and products they've used. The idea is to build a database of things vegan, facilitate this natural way of life and spread the good word! Whether you've been vegan all your life or you would like to cut down on the amount of meat you consume, this is a great site to be on!
